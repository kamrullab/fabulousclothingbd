About Us

Fabulous Clothing is a permanent business to business center located in the heart of Dhaka , Bangladesh for exportation of garments and leather products. Fabulous Clothing has been established with a team of smart and tremendously experienced professional Merchandisers, Commercials and Technically experts in fashion trade for Men, Women & Children garments for Knit, Woven and Sweaters as well. We act as an exporter of flawless ready-made garments for European & USA markets which inspire and energize the buyers. To keep our reputation unhurt, we have always stood for providing quality products, creating a new world of fashion and inviting the Buyers from all over the world to take part in our groovy collection of clothing. Besides, we focus sharply on our customers to provide excellent service, price and best delivery so that the customers could be more inspired towards strengthen a bilateral business relation.

Fabulous Clothing understands the latest international trends of fashion and the changes of design in fabrics and accessories often turn to new mode. The guys are working in the company, they are all more than 20 years experienced in the fashion trade by working practically with hundreds of reputed brand buyers through different big group of companies engaged in apparel export in Europe and the USA.

The Management of Fabulous Clothing is grave concerned and committed towards on time shipment of the merchandise with quality up to the mark as desired by the buyers.
